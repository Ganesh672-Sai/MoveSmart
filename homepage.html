Overview of Electric Autonomous Buses

Autonomous Buses: Autonomous buses use AI-powered systems, such as LiDAR, cameras, and radar, to navigate without human drivers. They can detect obstacles, monitor traffic conditions, and adjust speed in real time. These buses aim to reduce human errors, potentially lowering accident rates. Autonomous buses improve efficiency by optimizing routes and operating continuously. Trials are ongoing globally, but challenges like regulatory approval and infrastructure needs remain.
